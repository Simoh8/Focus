First things first
What is Body Doubling?
🧍‍♀️🧍‍♀️

Someone quietly sitting next to you while you work.

Body doubling is the simple act of having another person present while you do your tasks not to help, not to judge, just to be there. Like a friend sitting across from you at a coffee shop, both of you doing your own thing. Their presence keeps you grounded, focused, and less likely to spiral into distraction. It works even virtually.

In The Focus Tribe, you're never working alone. Every session, there are real people on the other side of the screen, doing their tasks alongside you. That shared energy is the magic.

Daily Check-in Days

On non-session days the community stays warm with a daily check-in. In the morning, Juddie posts one question in the group "What are you working on today?" and you share your intention for the day in the chat. In the evening there's a follow-up to celebrate what you got done. No calls, no pressure. Just a gentle nudge to keep showing up every day.

The process
How a Session Works
01

Join the Google Meet link

The link is pinned in the community. Show up at any session time cameras on or off, totally up to you.

02

Set your intention

At the start we go around quickly everyone shares the one thing they're working on this session. No long explanations needed.

03

Work alongside each other

Mics go on mute and everyone gets to work. The presence of other real people working is the magic it keeps you anchored and on track.

04

Check in & celebrate

At the end we share what we got done. Big or small it all counts. Acknowledging your wins is part of the process.

What's on the menu
Session Types
🎯

1hr Focus Session

A full hour of quiet, focused work. Set your task, show up, and get it done. Simple and powerful.

⏰

Anti-Procrastination

One hour dedicated specifically to that task you've been avoiding. We start it together so you don't have to start alone.

🍅

Pomodoro Session

Three focused 15-minute bursts with check-ins in between. Great when you need structure to stay on track.

🪜

Focus Ladder

One hour to break big overwhelming tasks into smaller steps. Ideal when you don't know where to start.

✅

Getting Things Done

A power hour for clearing your list. You come in with tasks and leave with ticks. Satisfying every time.

📅

Monday Special Rapid Wins + Week Planning

Every Monday we start the week right. First 30 minutes is Week Planning we map out priorities and set intentions. The next 30 minutes is Rapid Wins we knock out quick tasks to build momentum for the week ahead.
